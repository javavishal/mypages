Delete Your Account

Our app does not create or store user accounts. If you signed in with Google, your account remains with Google.

How to Remove Google Login Access

If you no longer want our app to access your Google account, follow these steps:

  1. Go to Google Account Permissions.
  2. Find our app in the list.
  3. Click "Remove Access".

If you have any questions, contact us at support@yourapp.com.
